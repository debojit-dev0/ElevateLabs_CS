1. **What makes a password strong?**

A strong password is long, unique, and uses uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

2. **What are common password attacks?**

Brute force attacks and dictionary attacks are the most common.

3. **Why is password length important?**

Longer passwords take much more time to crack, even with powerful computers.

4. **What is a dictionary attack?**

It tries common words and leaked passwords instead of random combinations.

5. **What is multi-factor authentication?**

It adds an extra step like OTP or fingerprint along with the password.

7. **What are passphrases?**

Passphrases are long combinations of words that are easy to remember but hard to crack.

Using short passwords, reusing passwords, and using personal information.
